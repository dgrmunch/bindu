# त्रिक · Trika

Trika is the triadic philosophical system of Kashmir Shaivism focusing on:
- Śiva (transcendent)
- Śakti (creative power)
- Nara (the individual)

Embraced within the Sarvāmnāya synthesis, especially in the Northern and Upper transmissions.
